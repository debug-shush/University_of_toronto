UNIVERSITY OF TORONTO

The University of Toronto (U of T), Canada is a global leader in higher education and research. Established in 1827 with its main campus at St. George, Downtown Toronto, Ontario - the university currently imparts world-class education at three different locations in Canada. With more than 980 programs at the undergraduate and graduate level, U of T offers a plethora of courses in an extensive range of disciplines. Whether you want to pursue engineering, arts, medicine, or social sciences, the University of Toronto, Canada has it all.
